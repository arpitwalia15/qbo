The Future of Edge Cloud Solutions

Transform your infrastructure that works on your terms

Schedule a Discovery Meeting

No-Compromise Metal Performance with QBO, Purpose-Built for AI at the Edge

QBO delivers metal-level performance with Kubernetes-in-Docker and Docker-in-Docker solutions, ensuring unmatched efficiency for AI workloads.

With QBO, all infrastructure dependencies—including DNS, Load Balancing, Firewall, Authentication, ACME, and more—are fully integrated, simplifying deployment and management.

Build GPU clouds with just servers, high-performance network cards, and switches, and at a fraction of the cost of hyperscalers.

Deploy on-prem, in the public cloud, at the edge, or in airgapped environments—with total control and flexibility.

Unlock the full potential of your AI innovations with QBO at the edge.

Experience the QBO Difference

Our edge cloud solutions provide enterprises with the scalability of public cloud, the control of private cloud, the flexibility of hybrid cloud, the cost efficiency of large capital expenditure (CapEx), and the agility of optimized operational expenditure (OpEx).

Learn More

Deploy Cloud Anywhere

TTurn any bare-metal server into a fully functional cloud platform, whether it’s: - On-premises for security and control. - At the edge for ultra-low latency and performance. - In a colocation facility to leverage dedicated infrastructure. - In a hybrid-cloud model to connect seamlessly across environments.

Scale Like Public Cloud, Control Like Private Cloud

Gain complete control while maintaining cloud-like scalability: - No virtualization overhead – Direct hardware access ensures peak performance. - Full workload isolation – Unlike shared infrastructure, every process runs independently. - Self-contained architecture – No reliance on external dependencies like DNS, firewalls, or load balancers.

The Best of CapEx and OpEx

Combine CapEx and OpEx strategies to reduce costs and optimize ROI: - Own hardware for long-term cost efficiency. - Scale on demand using flexible consumption models. - Pay for what you use without hyperscaler markups.

Avoid Vendor Lock-In with Open-Source Compatibility

QBO is CNCF Kubernetes-certified, meaning it runs Kubernetes-native applications without modification. Enterprises can: - Move workloads freely between cloud providers. - Avoid being locked into proprietary hyperscaler services. - Leverage open standards instead of vendor-controlled infrastructure.

Security Without Third-Party Risks

Secure your own infrastructure without compromise: - Workloads fully isolated at the process level. - Air-gapped deployments, secure and offline operation for highly regulated industries. - No reliance on third-party security layers—enterprises maintain full control.
